How to Buy

  1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.) to the website.
  2. Ensure you have sufficient funds (ETH, BNB, or supported tokens) in your wallet.
  3. Choose the amount of Zenthra tokens you wish to purchase during the presale.
  4. Confirm the transaction and wait for blockchain confirmation.
  5. Tokens will be automatically credited to your wallet once the transaction is successful.
